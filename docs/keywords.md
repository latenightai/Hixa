## Boolean Operators

| Assamese (Latin) | English Equivalent | Description/Usage                |
|------------------|-------------------|----------------------------------|
| **aru**          | and               | Logical and                      |
| **ba**           | or                | Logical or                       |
| **not_kora**     | not               | Logical not                      |

## Comparison Operators (Symbols)

These will use standard symbols:
- `==` for equality
- `!=` for not equal
- `>` for greater than
- `>=` for greater or equal
- `<` for less than
- `<=` for less or equal
